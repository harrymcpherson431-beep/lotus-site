The recovery app for people who'll never call a helpline.

Lotus turns the money you don't gamble into visible growth — a living lotus and real goals, built quietly, at your pace.

One email when Lotus launches. Maybe a short note once a month while it's being built. Nothing else, unsubscribe anytime.

Your bank stays yours.

Lotus never connects to your bank. Not optionally, not "coming soon" — the connection doesn't exist. You track manually; it sees only what you show it.

Safety is free. Forever.

The calm kit, the crisis pathway, tracking and your lotus will never be behind a paywall. This is a founding rule, not a pricing decision.

The lotus never dies.

No streaks. No zero. A relapse turns the water muddy — and the flower keeps growing. Recovery counted in growth, not in days you can lose.

How it works

1

Log what you didn't gamble. A skipped bet, a deposit not made — you enter it, by hand, in seconds.

2

Watch it become something. Every entry feeds your lotus and moves a real goal — flights, a licence, a buffer between you and the bad weeks.

3

Keep going, muddy water and all. Bad days are part of it. Nothing resets. The direction is the point.

Lotus is built by one person, from lived experience, in Australia — for anyone, anywhere, who wants out of the loop. It launches on iOS and Android in Australia, the UK, the US, Canada and New Zealand.
